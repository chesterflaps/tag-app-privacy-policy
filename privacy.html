Privacy Policy for TAG App

Last updated: July 2025

1. Introduction

TAG App ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use our TAG App service.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Contact Information

When you grant contacts permission, we collect:

  • Phone numbers from your contacts (only when you grant contacts permission)
  • No contact names or any other personal information from your contacts

2.2 Apple ID Information

When you sign in with Apple, we receive:

  • Your Apple ID email address (handled securely by Apple)

2.3 Device Information

We collect basic device information necessary for app functionality.

3. How We Use Your Information

3.1 Contact Data Usage

Phone numbers from your contacts are uploaded to our secure servers each time you access the contacts tab. This upload is required to:

  • Check for new TAGs that your contacts may have created
  • Verify TAG availability and status
  • Check if contacts have made their TAGs private
  • Provide real-time TAG information

This continuous checking is necessary for the app to function properly and provide you with up-to-date TAG information.

3.2 Apple ID Information

Your Apple ID information is handled securely by Apple and used only for authentication purposes.

3.3 Device Information

Basic device information is used for app functionality and service delivery.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your information:

  • All data is transmitted over secure, encrypted connections
  • Phone numbers are stored securely on our servers
  • We do not collect or store contact names or other personal information
  • Access to your data is strictly limited to what is necessary for the TAG service

5. Data Retention

We retain phone numbers only as long as necessary to provide the TAG service. You can request deletion of your data at any time through the app's settings.

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Withdraw consent for data processing
  • Contact us with privacy concerns

7. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Firebase - For app functionality and data storage
  • Apple Sign-In - For secure authentication

These services have their own privacy policies and security measures.

8. Children's Privacy

Our app is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page.

10. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:

  • Email: tagphoneapp@gmail.com
  • Support: Use the "Contact Support" feature in the app

11. Consent

By using TAG App, you consent to the collection and use of your information as described in this Privacy Policy. You can withdraw your consent at any time by deleting your account or contacting us.
